Emergency alert

This is an emergency alert issued by Cumbria Fire Service regarding Sellafield.

An Off-Site Radiation Emergency has been declared and the site siren is being sounded.

If you are within 8 kilometres of Sellafield, you must go indoors and shelter now.

Close all doors and windows and listen to BBC Radio Cumbria 104.1 FM for further information.

If you have a copy of the booklet, 'What To Do In An Emergency At Sellafield', read it now.

An Off-Site Radiation Emergency has been declared and the site siren is being sounded, if you are within 8 kilometres of Sellafield you must go indoors and shelter now.
