Emergency alert

This is an emergency alert issued by London Fire Service regarding severe flaoding at Walthamstow Reservoirs at 12:30PM on 28 Feb 2024.

Due to heavy rain and the failure of a reservoir wall, a large volume of flood water is overtopping the reservoirs at Forest Rd, N17 9NH.

The water is flowing outwards at speed into a wide area. The flood waters are deep (4-6 feet) and moving fast.

If you are within the N15, N16, N17, E5, E10 or E17 postcode areas, you must go indoors and shelter now.

Close windows and doors and block any other routes through which water may enter. If you can, turn off your electricity and gas supplies. If possible, go to an upper floor. Await further instructions from the emergency services.

Listen to BBC Radio London 94.9 FM for further information.
